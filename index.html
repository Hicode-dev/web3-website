About ROBNIL

Robben Island $ROBNIL is the World’s first decentralized charity and investment token, built on the binance network. $ROBNIL is designed to be self- sufficient, and a Decentralized Autonomous Foundation (DAF) making it one of the fastest-growing tokens, as it allows token holders to hold and trade and vote with their tokens as they will. This project is Inspired by the life and struggles of Nelson Mandela, $ROBNIL serves as a global financial platform for charitable services. Why The Robben Island foundation? We believe in the reformative system of approach where released persons can become anything, even Presidents. Our foundation will deepen relationship with various humanitarian charities around the world that are committed to helping tackle global prison and migration challenges.
